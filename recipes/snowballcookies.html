Snowball Cookies

Description

Snowball cookies are a delicious addition to your holiday must-have treats! Thanks to Spend with Pennies for this amazing recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Mix flour, walnuts/pecans, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat butter, vanilla, and powdered sugar together.
  4. Add the flour mixture to this bowl and beat on low.
  5. Form the dough into small balls and place on baking sheet lined with wax paper.
  6. Bake for about 10 minutes.
  7. After cookies have completely cooled, roll the cookies in the powdered sugar until they are completely covered.

Cauliflower Rice

Description

Cauliflower rice is a guilt-free way to enjoy rice without all the calories and carbs. It's easy and fast to make as well!

Ingredients

  • diced bell peppers
  • riced caulifower
  • olive oil or butter
  • eggs
  • chicken or fish

Steps

  1. Sautee the peppers in oil or butter in a pan on medium for about 10 minutes.
  2. Pour the riced caulifower (either fresh or frozen) into the same pan and mix with the peppers.
  3. In a separate pan, scramble several eggs. Set aside when done.
  4. Heat and dice chicken or fish. Set aside when done.
  5. When the caulifower has finished cooking (about 15 minutes or until soft), mix in the eggs and chicken or fish.
  6. Season to taste and enjoy!

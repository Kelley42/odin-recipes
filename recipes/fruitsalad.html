Fruit Salad

Description

Fruit salad is a wonderful meal for those hot summer days. It's cool and refreshing - and only takes a few minutes to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 apple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup seedless grapes
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 handful of walnut or pecan pieces
  • 2-3 tablespoons of cool whip
  • chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. Chop the apple, banana, and strawberries into small pieces.
  2. Mix the chopped fruit into a bowl with the grapes.
  3. Mix in the cool whip until each piece is slighty covered.
  4. Sprinkle in the walnuts or pecans. Toss slightly.
  5. Drizzle a tiny amount of chocolate syrup on top.
